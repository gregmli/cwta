Chen Zhenglei 2016 Seminar

The Light of Taichi Summer Camp

May 28th to June 1st, 2016 · Seattle, WA

The Chinese Wushu and Taichi Academy, in partnership with the Chen Zhenglei Taichi Training Center and Confucius Institution of the State of Washington, co-hosted a lecture by Grandmaster Chen Zhenglei at the University of Washington Kane Hall on June 1st. Check out photos of the event, including performances by Grandmaster Chen’s disciples and China’s Queen of Taichi, Master Gao Jiamin!
